# Choices Brand Book (v0.1 — "Final Cut")
> Consulting-style brand & market engagement, 2026-07-16. Full readout (interactive): https://claude.ai/code/artifact/3d48f73d-ccc3-4086-b3fe-7302489f9f09
> Thesis: **keep the name, change the fight** — win the group chat and For You page, not the App Store search box.

## Research findings (July 2026)
- **Name collision**: Pixelberry "Choices: Stories You Play" (~$544M lifetime, 1.3M+ ratings) owns exact-match "choices" search and the bare store name. Apple uniqueness is on the *full string*, so `Choices: <modifier>` is available; on-device name (`CFBundleDisplayName`) may legally stay plain "Choices".
- **Category clusters**: (a) wheel spinners — RNG without agency, ad-stuffed; (b) restaurant swipers — agency without play, DB-dependent; (c) couples apps (Paired/Flamme/Agapé) — play without utility, $60–120/yr, pastel/cozy. **No competitor found using turn-based elimination + no-blame outcome.** Whitespace: "playful adversarial game that outputs a real decision."
- **Visual confusion risks**: Pixelberry = teal, diamonds, script type, romance portraits. Spinners = rainbow wheels. Couples apps = pastel coral warmth. All on the avoid-list.

## Recommendations (proposed — need sign-off)
1. **Naming architecture**: App Store name **"Choices: Couples Food Game"** (26 chars; adds couples/food/game with zero subtitle overlap; runner-up "Choices: Dinner Decider"). Subtitle stays locked: "Decide what to eat, together". Keyword field draft (96/100): `dinner,decider,date,night,restaurant,picker,ideas,where,tonight,meal,swipe,match,fun,picky,eater`. Home screen / in-app / social stay plain **Choices**. Trademark: stylized wordmark+icon later, never the bare word.
2. **Visual identity "Final Cut"** (evolution of current system, not replacement):
   - Palette: Table Slate `#0B1020` canvas · Electric Indigo `#5B5BF7` primary (shifted off #4f46e5 / Paired-purple cluster) · **Winner Gold `#FFC53D`** (crown moment) · Cut Red `#FF4D5E` · Menu Paper `#F4F4F0`.
   - ⚑ **Touches shipped decisions**: gold replaces green `#22c55e` at the *brand* layer (green may stay as in-app success semantic); indigo hex nudge. Wife-approval gate applies.
   - Type: Archivo Black (display) + Space Grotesk (text) for marketing surfaces; in-app UI keeps system stack.
   - Icon: keep squircle 2×2-grid concept; rebuild in Icon Composer as layered single-silhouette glyph (3 struck squares + 1 gold crowned square) surviving iOS 26 Liquid Glass tinted/mono modes. No text.
   - Signature asset: **end-of-game share "cut card"** (Wordle-style result grid, red strikes + gold crown, "Tacos survived · nobody's fault") — build web-first.
   - Alternates documented in readout: B "Kitchen Bracket" (cream/tomato serif, light-mode), C "Cut Card" (lime-on-black result-first) — best ideas folded into Final Cut.
3. **Voice** (extends locked system): verb = "cut"; tease the situation never the partner; keep "Nobody picked this. Everybody picked this." and "{name} gave you Choices 😏 Your move →" verbatim. Never wellness-speak.
4. **Activation**: TikTok territories — "The last cut" (reveal endings), "The ritual" (origin story), "Strategic cuts" (game-theory banter). Screenshot storyboard: mid-game cut → gold reveal → PLUM-42 invite → premium tease → origin line; test top captions vs none.
